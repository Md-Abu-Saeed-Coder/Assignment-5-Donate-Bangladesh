What is a flood?

A flood is a natural disaster where water overflows onto normally dry land. This can happen due to heavy rainfall, ocean waves, rapid snowmelt, or the breaking of dams.

What is a flash flood?

A flash flood is a sudden and rapid flooding event, usually caused by heavy rainfall. It occurs quickly and provides little time for people to move to safety.

What should you do during a flood?

1.Move to higher ground.
2.Turn off electricity and gas connections.
3.Seek shelter in a safe place.

What should you do after a flood?
